A Bit About Me





Having done my schooling from New Delhi, India, I am a final year student at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. I am majoring in Computer Science and pursuing a minor in Business as well. As a Summer Tech Analyst at Goldman Sachs in 2017 I had the chance to work on front-end frameworks like React and data mining engines like ElasticSearch. In 2016 I developed a Windows 8 application as a Tech Intern for KPMG for ease of access to KPIs. I have also played with trending technologies as a Microsoft Student Partner and promoted my major as a Engineering Student Ambassador at HKUST.
